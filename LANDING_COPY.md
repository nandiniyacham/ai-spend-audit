## FAQ

### 1. Which AI tools are supported?

The platform currently supports ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Gemini, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, and other popular AI productivity tools.

### 2. How are savings calculated?

Savings are estimated using pricing comparisons, team-size fit analysis, redundant subscription detection, and lower-cost alternative recommendations.

### 3. Is my company information public?

No. Public report links remove identifying information such as company names and email addresses.

### 4. Do I need to connect billing accounts?

No. Users manually enter their current plans, seat counts, and monthly spending.

### 5. Who is this tool built for?

The tool is designed primarily for startups, small engineering teams, freelancers, and AI-heavy developer workflows.
